I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the DART group of the Department of Computer Science of NTNU. I hold a research fellowship from ERCIM. Before, I was a postdoctoral grantee at the Instituto Superior Técnico in 2016 and 2018-2019.

My main research interests are planning under uncertainty, in particular using decision-theory methods such as Partially Observable Markov Decision Processes (POMDPs)
